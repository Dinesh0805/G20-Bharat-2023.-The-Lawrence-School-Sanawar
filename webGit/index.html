ONE EARTH - ONE FAMILY - ONE FUTURE

G20 BHARAT 2023


India

India's vision for the G20 summit revolves around inclusivity and sustainable development, encapsulated by the motto 'Vasudhaiva Kutumbakam,' which means 'the world is one family.'


Indonesia

Against this backdrop, Indonesia G20 Presidency laid out three priorities, namely (1) global health architecture, (2) sustainable energy transition, and (3) digital transformation, under the overarching theme of “Recover Together, Recover Stronger”.


Saudi Arabia

The Kingdom would guide the work of the G20 under the theme of “Realizing Opportunities of the 21st Century for All” and would officially focus on three aims: Empowering People by creating the conditions in which all people – especially women and youth – can live, work and thrive.


Russia

Russia, as this year's chair, hosted the G20 finance ministers and central bank governors' meeting in Moscow on 15 and 16 February 2013


France

French President Emmanuel Macron will arrive in New Delhi on Saturday for a two-day visit to attend the G20 Summit under India's Presidency scheduled to be held from September 9-10.


South Africa

South Africa will be joining the G20 troika along with India and Brazil this year and it will take up the G20’s presidency in 2025. G20 troika is a three-member group of past, current and succeeding presidencies.


South Korea

In particular, the main theme of the 2018 G20 Summit was in line with the Korean government's vision of an inclusive state where all people prosper together.


United States

Several nations at the G20 summit, including the US, India and Saudi Arabia, announced plans to set up rail and port links between the Middle East and South Asia and eventually to Europe, a move which US President Joe Biden said was a “real big deal”.


Germany

The 20 Heads of State and Government and top-level representatives of international organizations will gather under the motto of Germany's G20 Presidency: 'Shaping an interconnected world'.


Italy

Under the leadership of Italy, the Ministers of Culture of the G20 group of the world's largest economies agreed on 30 July 2021 for the first time in history on a G20 Declaration on Culture that firmly positions culture as a major engine for sustainable socio-economic recovery in the wake of the COVID-19 pandemic.


Türkiye

To this end, during its communication programme , Turkey used the motto “G-20. Together for Inclusive and Robust Growth”.


Japan

Discussions under the Japanese Presidency is made under the following three pillars: (1) implementation of G20 commitments including those related to women's labour participation, (2) enhancing support for girls' and women's education including STEM (Science, Technology, Engineering and Mathematics) area


Canada

Canada played a leading role in the creation of the G20 and was instrumental in setting up the first meeting of G20 leaders in 2008.


Australia

Australia works constructively with other G20 economies in pursuit of strong, sustainable, balanced and inclusive global economic growth. Within the forum, Australia actively promotes an open global economy and a rules-based multilateral trading system.


China

China in the past couple of months has started opposing many Indian proposals in the language of the final ‘Outcome Document’ or ‘Joint Communique’ which requires consensus. Bringing out a consensus document has become the biggest diplomatic challenge for India.


United Kingdom

United Kingdom Prime Minister Rishi Sunak on Friday shared his profound experience of interacting with young students at the British Council in Delhi, who is in national captial for the G20 Summit on September 9, 10. Describing the students as the "world leaders of tomorrow," Sunak conveyed his optimism about the global future.


Mexico

The UK is correctly known as the United Kingdom of Great Britain and Northern Ireland. It consists of four parts: England, Scotland, Wales and Northern Ireland. Alongside English, Welsh, Gaelic and Cornish are spoken.


Argentina

The overarching theme of the G20 under Argentina's Presidency was 'Building Consensus For Fair And Sustainable Development'. The priority areas for Argentina's Presidency were - Future of work; Fourth Industrial Revolution, Health, Youth, Infrastructure for development; and Sustainable food security.


Brazil

Brazil President Lula da Silva also announced that these priorities are part of the Brazilian presidency motto which says 'Building a fair world and a sustainable planet'.


European Union

The European Union is a full member of the G20 , alongside three of its Member States who are also permanent members: France, Germany, and Italy.
